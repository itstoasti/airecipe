Yummy - AI Recipe Generator

Data Deletion Request

You can delete your personal data while keeping your Yummy account active. This allows you to start fresh while maintaining your account and subscription.

Note: This option deletes your data but keeps your account. If you want to delete both your account and all data, please visit our Account Deletion page.

What Data Can Be Deleted?

You have control over the following data types:

Saved Recipes

All recipes you've saved to your collection. You can delete individual recipes or all of them.

Recipe Generation History

Your history of generated recipes and AI interactions.

Shopping Lists

All shopping lists you've created from recipes.

Usage Data

App usage statistics and preferences (excluding necessary account data).

⚠️ Warning: Deleting your data is permanent and cannot be undone. Make sure to export or save any recipes you want to keep before proceeding.

How to Delete Your Data

Option 1: Delete Specific Data in the App

To delete individual recipes:

  1. Open the Yummy app
  2. Go to Saved Recipes
  3. Swipe left on any recipe
  4. Tap Delete

To delete all saved recipes:

  1. Go to your Profile
  2. Tap Settings
  3. Scroll to Data Management
  4. Tap "Clear All Saved Recipes"
  5. Confirm your decision

Option 2: Request Complete Data Deletion

To delete all your data while keeping your account, send an email to:

Email: support@yummyapp.com

Please include:

  • The email address associated with your account
  • Subject line: "Data Deletion Request"
  • Specify which data you want deleted
  • Confirm you want to keep your account active

We will process your request within 30 days and send you a confirmation email.

What Happens After Data Deletion?

  • Your selected data will be permanently deleted from our servers within 30 days
  • Your account will remain active
  • Your subscription status will not be affected
  • You can continue using the app and generating new recipes
  • Some anonymized data may be retained for legal compliance or analytics

Data That Cannot Be Deleted (While Keeping Account)

The following data is required to maintain your account and cannot be deleted separately:

  • Email address (account identifier)
  • Account creation date
  • Subscription status and payment history (managed by Google Play)

To delete this data, you must delete your entire account. Visit our Account Deletion page for more information.
