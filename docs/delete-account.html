Account Deletion Request

We're sorry to see you go. This page explains how to permanently delete your Yummy account and all associated data.

⚠️ Warning: This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

Deleting your account will permanently remove:

  • Your account credentials and profile
  • All saved recipes
  • Recipe generation history
  • Shopping lists
  • All personal data associated with your account

How to Delete Your Account

Option 1: Delete from the App

  1. Open the Yummy app
  2. Go to your Profile (bottom navigation)
  3. Scroll down to the bottom
  4. Tap "Delete Account"
  5. Confirm your decision

Your account and all data will be deleted immediately.

Option 2: Email Request

If you cannot access the app, send an email to:

Email: support@yummyapp.com

Please include:

  • The email address associated with your account
  • Subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  • Confirmation that you want to delete your account and all data

We will process your request within 30 days and send you a confirmation email.

What happens after deletion?

  • Your account will be immediately deactivated
  • All personal data will be permanently deleted from our servers within 30 days
  • Some anonymized usage statistics may be retained for legal or analytical purposes
  • Active subscriptions will be canceled (refunds follow Google Play policy)

Just want to delete your data?

If you want to delete your data but keep your account, please visit our Data Deletion page.

Need Help?

If you have questions or concerns, please contact us:

Email: support@yummyapp.com

We typically respond within 24-48 hours.
