Privacy Policy

Yummy - AI Recipe Generator

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Yummy. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Email address and password when you create an account
  • Recipe Data: Recipes you generate, save, and create within the app
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the app, including recipe generation history
  • Subscription Data: Payment information processed through RevenueCat and Google Play (we do not store payment details)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain our services
  • Generate personalized AI-powered recipes
  • Manage your account and subscriptions
  • Send service-related notifications
  • Improve our app and user experience
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is securely stored using Supabase, a trusted cloud database provider. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or destruction.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Database and authentication services
  • OpenAI: AI recipe generation
  • FAL.ai: Recipe image generation
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management
  • Google Play: Payment processing

These services have their own privacy policies governing their use of your information.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. You may request deletion of your data at any time.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Request deletion of your account
  • Opt-out of marketing communications

8. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date."
